About Me

Background

Hello and welcome to my simple personal website :) first of all, thank you for checking out my personal website. I am a Computer Science Student at King's College London currently studying at a 3rd-year level. On this website I will be telling you a bit about myself and what I do.

My interest in engineering started when I was in high school when I enrolled in International Baccalaureate (IB) program. IB is equivalent to A-levels in the UK and it is a very challenging program. I undertook Maths, Computer Science, and Economics at a higher level; and English, Turkish, and Biology at a standard level. Then I pursued a degree in Computer Science at King's College London.

I started my studies in 2020 and have been in London ever since. Since I started studying, I have undertaken courses that enhanced my knowledge of software development. My classes allowed me to explore different ranges of software including low-level programs like Operating Systems to high-level programs like Web development. During this time I also focused on my self-development as a developer and built open what's thought in school. Below you may find more details about my technical expertise.
